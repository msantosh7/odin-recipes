How to cook lasagna in 5 min

Description

This recipe will take just 5 min as mentioned in the title. All you need is a microwave oven and ready to cook lasagna.

Now that you know the required items, below I'll be describing the recipe

Ingredients

  • Microwave Oven
  • Ready to cook lasagna

Steps:

  1. Check the power o/p of your oven. This recipe is for 1000W ovens. Scale the times accordingly.
  2. Read the instructions on the packet and follow them.
  3. Don't forget to clean the oven, which BTW no one does(including me).
